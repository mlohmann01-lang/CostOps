# Operational Scoring Model

Deterministic scoring (no AI-generated score values):
- Evidence Confidence
- Readiness Score
- Authority Coverage
- Drift Stability
- Verification Maturity
- Overall Governance Confidence

Scores are derived from recommendation verdict, blast/rollback risk, and authority evidence availability.

Flexera authority can adjust confidence upward (validation) or downward (mismatch/blocker) with explicit reason text.
