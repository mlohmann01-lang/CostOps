# kubernetes-economics-recon
## CURRENT_CLOUD_ECONOMIC_CAPABILITIES
Cloud economic intelligence exists for normalized resource economics and governance recommendations.
## CURRENT_COMMITMENT_ECONOMICS_CAPABILITIES
Commitment economics layer exists for utilization, coverage, decay, elasticity, and expiration governance.
## KUBERNETES_COST_MODELING_GAPS
Kubernetes namespace/workload/pod/node-pool/autoscaling economics were not modeled deterministically.
## CLUSTER_SIGNAL_REQUIREMENTS
Cluster, node pool, autoscaling, GPU, and utilization/cost signals with lineage and replay IDs.
## NAMESPACE_SIGNAL_REQUIREMENTS
Namespace ownership, environment, cost-allocation, and shared-cost ambiguity indicators.
## WORKLOAD_RIGHTSIZING_LIMITATIONS
Request/usage quality and production criticality limit aggressive recommendation confidence.
## AUTOSCALING_ECONOMICS_DEPENDENCIES
HPA/VPA alignment, floor replicas, scale-in behavior, cron/batch scaling patterns.
## GPU_WORKLOAD_DEPENDENCIES
GPU request/usage, idle time, burst profiles, commitment alignment.
## EPHEMERAL_WORKLOAD_RISKS
Job/cron sprawl, stale preview environments, and cleanup policy drift.
## INTEGRATION_WITH_FINOPS_OPERATIONALIZATION
Produces governance-safe review recommendations and waste/risk metrics.
## INTEGRATION_WITH_ENTERPRISE_DECISION_SYSTEM
Feeds deterministic cluster economics for simulation and executive tradeoff decisions.
## NON_NEGOTIABLE_BOUNDARIES
No Kubernetes/cloud mutation; no execution/telemetry/replay/workflow/UI expansion.
