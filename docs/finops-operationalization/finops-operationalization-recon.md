# finops-operationalization-recon

## CURRENT_FINOPS_MARKET_CAPABILITIES
- Visibility, recommendation generation, allocation, and workflow ticketing are mature.

## FLEXERA_APPTIO_SERVICENOW_SUPPORT_MODEL
- External systems provide source recommendations and workflow context; this layer operationalizes governance and realization proof.

## OPERATIONALIZATION_GAPS
- Evidence/owner/readiness normalization and realization-proof loops are inconsistent in typical FinOps flows.

## SAVINGS_EXECUTION_FAILURE_MODES
- Missing owners, incomplete evidence, high-risk changes without approvals, reversal and recurrence drift.

## OWNER_ACCOUNTABILITY_DEPENDENCIES
- Tags, cost centers, assignment groups, cloud/subscription/app/business/technical owner hints.

## SERVICE_NOW_WORKFLOW_DEPENDENCIES
- Work package preparation only; no direct ServiceNow mutation.

## FORECASTING_DEPENDENCIES
- Economic forecasting, simulation, calibration, and memory signals.

## REALIZATION_PROOF_DEPENDENCIES
- Outcome tracking + realization vs expected measurements.

## RECURRENCE_PREVENTION_REQUIREMENTS
- Policy recommendations for owner, tagging, TTL, anomaly, commitment and lifecycle recurrence controls.

## NON_NEGOTIABLE_BOUNDARIES
- No auto-execution/remediation; no cloud/vendor/budget mutation; no execution/replay/telemetry/workflow subsystem fork; no UI expansion.
