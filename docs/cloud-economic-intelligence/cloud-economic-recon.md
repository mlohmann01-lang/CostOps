# cloud-economic-recon

## CURRENT_FINOPS_OPERATIONALIZATION_CAPABILITIES
Existing FinOps layer normalizes recommendations, readiness, accountability, forecasts, and policy hints.
## CURRENT_CLOUD_ECONOMIC_GAPS
Missing deterministic cloud-resource economics and drift-focused risk scoring.
## CLOUD_RESOURCE_MODEL_REQUIREMENTS
Provider/resource/environment/cost/owner/utilization/workload evidence model with replay and lineage IDs.
## UTILIZATION_SIGNAL_DEPENDENCIES
CPU/memory/storage/network/GPU utilization, active-idle hours, request activity.
## IDLE_CONFIDENCE_LIMITATIONS
Low evidence freshness, volatile workloads, and weak ownership reduce idle certainty.
## WORKLOAD_VOLATILITY_REQUIREMENTS
Seasonality/burstiness/scheduled-batch indicators to prevent false idle classification.
## AUTOSCALING_WASTE_DEPENDENCIES
Scaling floor/ceiling behavior, reserved capacity posture, threshold alignment signals.
## ENVIRONMENT_LIFECYCLE_REQUIREMENTS
Environment type, TTL tags, always-on patterns, weekend inactivity.
## STORAGE_AND_TRANSFER_ECONOMIC_RISKS
Snapshot staleness, unattached storage, retention sprawl, egress/cross-region concentration.
## INTEGRATION_WITH_FINOPS_OPERATIONALIZATION
Produces recommendation-ready governance artifacts; no execution payloads.
## INTEGRATION_WITH_ENTERPRISE_DECISION_SYSTEM
Feeds deterministic waste/risk/readiness indicators for executive decisions.
## NON_NEGOTIABLE_BOUNDARIES
RECOMMEND_ONLY and APPROVAL_REQUIRED modes only; no direct cloud/vendor mutation.
