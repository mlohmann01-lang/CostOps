# Enterprise Decision Recon
## CURRENT_EXECUTIVE_GOVERNANCE_CAPABILITIES
Executive governance provides risk posture, strategy comparison, readiness scoring, and deterministic executive report outputs for governance-first decision support.
## CURRENT_FORECASTING_SIMULATION_CALIBRATION_MEMORY_CAPABILITIES
Forecasting, simulation, calibration, and memory modules provide confidence, reliability, and scenario signals reusable as evidence-linked inputs.
## CURRENT_FINOPS_OPERATIONALIZATION_CAPABILITIES
FinOps operationalization provides owner accountability, recurrence prevention, readiness, and savings action planning that informs sequencing and governance prerequisites.
## ENTERPRISE_DECISION_GAPS
Missing canonical enterprise-wide prioritization, strategy arbitration, governance prerequisite gating, and allocation advisory in one deterministic report.
## ECONOMIC_PRIORITIZATION_DEPENDENCIES
Depends on evidence quality, forecast confidence, memory reliability, and operational readiness from existing authority modules.
## STRATEGY_ARBITRATION_REQUIREMENTS
Must arbitrate acceleration vs governance safety and short-term containment vs durable transformation under risk-adjusted scoring.
## SEQUENCING_DEPENDENCIES
Requires deterministic prerequisite ordering: ownership/data trust/governance before acceleration and broad transformation.
## GOVERNANCE_PREREQUISITE_DEPENDENCIES
Requires owner clarity, approval readiness, data trust, calibration/memory reliability, and operationalization maturity.
## DECISION_CONFIDENCE_LIMITATIONS
Confidence degrades under weak evidence, volatile signals, and high decision complexity; no opaque ML scoring is permitted.
## NON_AUTONOMOUS_DECISION_BOUNDARIES
Module is read/recommend/approval-only and provides no direct execution, remediation, procurement, or mutation actions.
## NON_NEGOTIABLE_BOUNDARIES
No AUTO_EXECUTE/AUTO_REMEDIATE, no ERP/ServiceNow/vendor/cloud/SaaS mutation, no execution/replay/telemetry/workflow/UI subsystem expansion.
