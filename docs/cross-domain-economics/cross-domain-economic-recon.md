# cross-domain-economic-recon

## CURRENT_DOMAIN_CAPABILITIES
M365 and AI Economics domains both emit governed recommendation candidates routed through decision intelligence-like flows.
## CROSS_DOMAIN_SIGNAL_SURFACES
Shared signals: user/team activity, tool usage, spend, productivity outcomes, governance risk and recommendation outcomes.
## CORRELATION_DEPENDENCIES
Correlation depends on tenantId alignment, entity identity normalization, evidence references, and recency windows.
## DUPLICATION_RISK_AREAS
Overlap across Copilot/Cursor/ChatGPT/Claude, premium M365 + external AI tooling, and duplicate savings claims.
## OUTCOME_ATTRIBUTION_RISK_AREAS
Double counting across AI+SaaS productivity claims and weak evidence for realized outcomes.
## GOVERNANCE_RISK_AREAS
Shadow AI with enterprise data, weak ownership clarity, high spend with weak governance controls.
## ARBITRATION_REQUIREMENTS
Suppress duplicates, escalate high governance risk, prefer higher-confidence outcome-backed recommendations.
## OUTCOME_LEDGER_REQUIREMENTS
Preserve replay/lineage IDs, confidence, and double-count risk markers without creating a new ledger subsystem.
## NON_NEGOTIABLE_BOUNDARIES
No AUTO_EXECUTE/AUTO_REMEDIATE, no vendor mutation, no subsystem forks, no UI expansion.
