# CONNECTOR OPERATIONALIZATION

## Why it exists
Protect enterprise operations with controlled governance and outcome proof.

## Architecture
Layered services over canonical state with strict boundaries.

## Workflows
Ingest -> assess -> govern -> execute safely -> verify outcomes.

## Safety rules
No cross-tenant access, no bypass of trust/governance, no uncontrolled execution.

## Operational behavior
Deterministic, tenant-scoped, auditable.

## Future roadmap
Expand production hardening and partner operationalization depth.
