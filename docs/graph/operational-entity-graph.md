# Operational Entity Graph

Deterministic, evidence-derived graph intelligence only.

## Rules
- Relationships are evidence-derived, provenance-tagged, confidence-scored.
- No opaque AI linking, no source-data mutation, no governance bypass.
- Correlation is replay-safe via deterministic hashes and versioned snapshots.

## Confidence
Entity/relationship confidence incorporates identifier quality, trust, and source reliability.

## Orphan/Duplicate
Orphans and duplicate candidates are flagged for review only; no auto-merge.
