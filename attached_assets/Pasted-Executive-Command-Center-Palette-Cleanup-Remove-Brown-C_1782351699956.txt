Executive Command Center Palette Cleanup — Remove Brown Cast

Do not change layout, tests, routes, components, or business logic.

Goal: remove the remaining brown/bronze tint from the platform UI. Keep gold as an accent only. Surfaces should feel charcoal/black, not brown.

## Replace brown surfaces

Search for and remove/replace colours like:

```css
rgba(245,196,81,0.08)
rgba(245,196,81,0.10)
rgba(245,196,81,0.12)
rgba(245,196,81,0.14)
rgba(245,196,81,0.18)
rgba(245,196,81,0.22)
rgba(245,196,81,0.30)
rgba(245,196,81,0.35)
```

Do not remove them everywhere — only where they are used as **background fills**.

Use gold for:

* borders
* icons
* small badges
* active indicators
* sparklines
* chart bars
* key numbers

Do not use gold/brown for:

* large card backgrounds
* page section backgrounds
* sidebar item backgrounds
* alert panel fills
* full-width banners

## Preferred surface colours

Use charcoal surfaces:

```css
--surface-base: #050607;
--surface-panel: #0B0D0F;
--surface-card: #101316;
--surface-card-hover: #14181C;
--surface-elevated: #171A1F;
```

## Gold usage

Use restrained gold:

```css
--gold: #F5C451;
--gold-bright: #FFCC4D;
--gold-border: rgba(245,196,81,0.36);
--gold-glow: rgba(245,196,81,0.18);
```
