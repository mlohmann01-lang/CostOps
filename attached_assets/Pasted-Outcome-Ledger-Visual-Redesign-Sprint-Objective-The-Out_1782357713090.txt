Outcome Ledger Visual Redesign Sprint

## Objective

The Outcome Ledger is now backed by the canonical ledger model.

This sprint is **presentation only**.

Do not redesign the data model.

Do not add new business logic.

Do not duplicate calculations.

The page must become the executive authority for answering one question:

> **Can you prove the value delivered by every technology decision?**

The design should feel like an executive finance console, not a report or database table.

---

# Core Design Principle

The Outcome Ledger is **not** a savings report.

It is the **financial chain of custody for technology value**.

Every optimisation is treated as a governed financial asset moving through its lifecycle:

```text
Projected
      ↓
Approved
      ↓
Executed
      ↓
Verified
      ↓
Finance Confirmed
      ↓
Protected
```

Every transition is backed by evidence.

Every value movement is explainable.

# Visual Language

Continue using the Economic Gold theme.

Rules:

Dark charcoal surfaces.

* value
* active
* evidence
* finance

Green:

* verified
* protected

Red:

* failed
* drift
* high leakage

Grey:

* pending
* unreached

Do not introduce brown backgrounds.
